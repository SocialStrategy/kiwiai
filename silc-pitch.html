We solved this problem for a firm like yours.

A Dutch financial advisory firm needed to process quarterly reports for 200 portfolio companies. Every quarter, someone downloaded each report, skimmed hundreds of pages, extracted the key data points by hand, and wrote a summary. The work was reliable. It was also brutal. It consumed two-thirds of their available manpower every cycle.

What we built

An AI system that automatically detects and downloads every quarterly report as it lands, extracts 20+ structured data points from each document, indexes everything into a searchable knowledge base, and generates bespoke summary reports from the extracted data. The analysts stopped skimming PDFs and started working with the data directly.

Outcome: Two-thirds of the quarterly workload eliminated.
